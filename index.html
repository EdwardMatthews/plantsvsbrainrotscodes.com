Essential Gameplay Tips for Plants vs Brainrots

Early Game Strategy

Start with affordable plants like Eggplant and Watermelon. Focus on building multiple garden rows and accumulating cash before investing in rare plants. Use the initial $400 wisely to establish a strong foundation.

Optimal Plant Placement

Arrange plants strategically across all lanes to maximize damage output. Avoid concentrating all plants in one row, as bosses can break through weak points. Distribute firepower evenly for better defense.

Boss Preparation

Before reaching 500 spawns (when the first boss appears), ensure all lanes have sufficient firepower. Mythical or higher-tier plants with mutations provide significant advantages in boss battles.

Fusion Priority

After your first Rebirth, focus on the Fusion Machine. Combine high-rarity plants with specific Brainrots to create powerful fused units. Popular combinations include Watermelon + Bombini for explosive damage.

Resource Management

Balance spending between new seeds, garden plots, and platform expansions. Defeated Brainrots generate passive income - a Normal Noobini Bananini produces $2/second, while Gold variants yield $8/second.

Mutation Benefits

Plants have a chance to mutate when planted, gaining unique visual effects and increased stats. Higher Luck increases mutation chances. Use Lucky Potions from codes to boost your odds.
