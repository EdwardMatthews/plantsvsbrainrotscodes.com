How to Fuse Plants vs Brainrots Components

Operating the PvB fuse machine successfully means understanding exactly how to fuse Plants vs Brainrots units without losing resources to bugs or mistakes.

Add the matching brainrot to fuse Plants vs Brainrots components together. Each PvB fuse recipe has fixed pairings - you can't substitute when you fuse.

Plants vs Brainrots fuse times range from 3-5 minutes. Premium PvB fuse results like Los Mr Carrotitos take the full 5 minutes.

Receive Your Fusion

Your Plants vs Brainrots fuse result appears in inventory automatically. Verify the PvB fuse succeeded before starting your next fusion.

⚠️ Critical Fusion Warnings

  • Inventory Overflow = Permanent Loss: Full inventory when Plants vs Brainrots fuse completes deletes your result forever. This is the worst PvB fuse mistake possible.
  • Mutations Don't Transfer: When you fuse Plants vs Brainrots mutated units, the PvB fuse result is always the base version without mutations.
  • Size is Random: Large components don't mean large results when you fuse Plants vs Brainrots units. PvB fuse size is always random.
  • Components Are Consumed: Plants vs Brainrots fuse process destroys both inputs permanently. Once you fuse, there's no undo.
  • One Fusion at a Time: The single Plants vs Brainrots fuse machine means one PvB fuse at a time, bottlenecking mass production.

PvB fuse never transfers mutations by design. Every Plants vs Brainrots fuse produces base versions. Don't fuse mutated units unless the PvB fuse income beats their current value.
