Your Privacy Matters

Plants vs Brainrots Codes operates the website https://plantsvsbrainrotscodes.com as a free informational resource for game players. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding information collection and use when you visit our website.

We built this site with privacy in mind. You can browse our entire website without creating an account, without providing personal information, and without any registration requirements. We believe game guides should be freely accessible to everyone.

Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many websites, we intentionally avoid collecting personal information:

  • No account registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or personal identifiers requested
  • No payment information processed
  • No user profiles or accounts created
  • No social media login requirements
  • No location tracking implemented

You remain anonymous while using our website. We cannot identify individual visitors because we don't collect identifying information.

Automatic Technical Information

When you visit any website, certain technical information is automatically exchanged between your browser and the web server. This standard web traffic data includes:

Server Logs

Our web hosting provider automatically records standard server logs that may include:

  • IP address (your internet connection identifier)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time and date of visits
  • Referring website (where you came from)
  • Operating system

This technical data helps us understand traffic patterns, detect security threats, and ensure website stability. Server logs are typically retained for 30-90 days by our hosting provider and then automatically deleted.

Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. These tools process data in aggregate form, meaning we see overall patterns rather than individual behavior. Analytics help us identify popular content, broken links, and areas for improvement.

Our analytics configuration:

  • IP addresses are anonymized
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No user profiling
  • Data retained for maximum 26 months
  • Aggregated reports only

Cookies and Local Storage

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. Our cookie usage is minimal and focused on functionality:

Essential Cookies

We may use essential cookies for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Maintaining your language selection
  • Preserving your content filter choices during your session

Analytics Cookies

Analytics services may set cookies to:

  • Distinguish unique visitors
  • Calculate visitor, session, and campaign data
  • Track site usage for internal reports

Your Cookie Control

You control all cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Receive warnings before accepting cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only

Blocking cookies won't prevent you from using our website, though some convenience features may not work optimally.

Third-Party Services

Our website may include content from third-party services that have their own privacy practices:

External Links

  • Roblox game pages

Content Delivery Network (CDN)

We use CDN services to deliver website assets quickly. CDNs may process your IP address to serve content from the nearest geographic server, improving page load speeds.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect our website infrastructure:

  • HTTPS encryption for all pages
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure hosting environment
  • Protection against common web attacks
  • Regular backups for disaster recovery

Since we don't collect personal information, there's minimal risk of personal data exposure. The primary security focus is maintaining website availability and preventing unauthorized modifications.

Children's Privacy

Our website provides game guides for a Roblox game that may be played by users under 13. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13
  • No registration or personal information required from any age group
  • Content is appropriate for all ages
  • No interactive features that could expose children to risks
  • No ability to upload content or communicate with others through our site

Parents can feel confident allowing children to use our website for game information. If you believe we've inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately for removal.

  • Technical data processing occurs in the United States

California Residents
