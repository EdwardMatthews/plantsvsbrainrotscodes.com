About Plants vs Brainrots

Plants vs Brainrots (PvB) is a tower defense game on Roblox where players strategically place plants to defend against waves of brain rot enemies. Created by Yo Gurt Studio, the game features unique mechanics including plant fusion, rebirth systems, and epic boss battles. This wiki covers all game elements to help players progress efficiently.
